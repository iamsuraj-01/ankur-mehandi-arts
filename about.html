About Us

12 Years of Expertise in Mehendi and Tattoo Artistry

Founded by Ankur Prajapati in 2012, Ankur Mehandi Arts has been a trusted name in Rishikesh for stunning mehendi and tattoo artistry. With 12 years of experience, we cater to weddings, festivals, birthday parties, kitty parties, and various functions, ensuring every design reflects beauty and precision. Whether it's exquisite bridal mehendi, intricate festival patterns, or stunning tattoos, we bring your ideas to life with professionalism and creativity.
